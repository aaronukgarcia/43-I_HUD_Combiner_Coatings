## Overview

Proposes polymer multilayer films as narrowband reflective coatings for HUD and helmet-mounted display combiners. Three candidate architectures: BCP self-assembled, co-extruded, and laminated polymer films. Whether any can achieve HUD-grade spectral precision is the central open question.

## Key Idea

A HUD combiner reflects display light at specific wavelengths while transmitting ambient light â€” this is the general class of optical behaviour that structural-colour polymer films produce. Polymer films could offer conformability and roll-to-roll cost advantages over traditional vacuum-deposited inorganic coatings.
